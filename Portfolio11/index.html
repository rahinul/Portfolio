WHO WE ARE

At Concept to Harvest, our goal is to provide first class opportunities to investors looking to
get involved in the exciting and growing cannabis industry.


By creating strong relationships with fellow cannabusiness owners, we understand the needs
as the industry comes out of the shadows and into the legalized world of business.


Our extensive experience in running a successful technology firm since 1995 AND building
a cannabusiness from the ground up in 2016, gives us a unique and commanding position to
understand the cannabis industry.


Like most of the best jungle guides, we’ve experienced the pitfalls and we’ve learned where
most make the costly mistakes. Let us help you steer clear of the endless money pits.
Talk to us.

leadership

sampleprojects

Three Finger Farm

A top shelf, blue ribbon winning grower with tons of talent and
potential! Their growing capabilities are among the elite in the
cannabis industry. Concept to Harvest sat down with them to talk
about what their plans were and the current state of their endeavor.
Being growers who’ve always worked in the shadows since the early
90’s, they were new to the ideas and proven practices of the high
end business operations of a legitimate corporation. Concept to
Harvest came in, partnered with TFF, remodeled the organization,
then implemented a new corporate structure and accounting
practices, saving a multi-award winning grower from having to start
all over, to today enjoying profit, awards and positive press!

AmberLight Dispensary

One of Portland’s most beautiful
dispensaries, Amber Light was
struggling behind the scenes. After
hearing, first-hand, how Concept to
Harvest helped other Cannabusinesses,
they turned to CtH when one of their
founding partners wanted to go a
different direction. CtH stepped in,
partnered with the remaining partners
and restructured the dispensary to
enjoy efficiency and profit!
